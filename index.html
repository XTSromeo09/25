Fortnite

Genre: Battle Royale

Gameplay: Players are dropped onto an island and must be the last person or team standing. They gather resources, build structures, and engage in combat. A shrinking play zone forces players into closer proximity, intensifying battles.

Rainbow Six Siege

Genre: Tactical Shooter

Gameplay: Teams of operators with unique abilities engage in intense, close-quarters combat. Players strategize, breach structures, and use gadgets to achieve objectives. Destruction of the environment is a key feature, allowing for dynamic and unpredictable gameplay.

Call of Duty

Genre: First-Person Shooter

Gameplay: Offers various game modes, including traditional team deathmatch, objective-based missions, and battle royale. Fast-paced action, realistic weapons, and a focus on individual skill characterize the gameplay.
